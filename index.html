Blackjack

Basic Rules
  • Dealer stands on 17; Dealer hits on 16 and below

    --George: Do we want people to know this???

  • Win 2x bet payout | Natural Blackjack 2.5x bet payout | Natural Blackjack beats unnatural 21, and pushes with a dealer's natural
  • Double downs allowed
  • No splits allowed
  • If a Double Down is pushed, only the original amount is pushed, the rest is returned.
  • House tax of 5% on winnings

Minimum bet: 50k

Maximum bet: 250k

VIPs: Max 500k bet

The player starts by placing a bet.

After collecting all bets, the house will deal two(2) cards for each player and one(1) card for the house.

Next, each player will be individually asked whether they choose to stay with their two(2) cards or hit (have the house deal them another card) - there's no limit on the number of hits that a player can ask for. A players turn is over when they stay or bust.

After every player has settled with their final hand, the house will now deal for themselves. When the house count is under 16, the house is forced to draw another card.

Results are then announced and rewards are distributed for the winners.

In the case of a tie, the bet is pushed to the next round, unless the player asks for it back or wants to increase/decrease it.

To deal a card, the dealer will roll a /dice 13.

  • 1 = Ace (1 or 11 decided anytime to keep the hand from busting).
  • 2-10 = Numbered cards. They represent their respective values.
  • 11 = Jack. Valued at 10
  • 12 = Queen. Valued at 10
  • 13 = King. Valued at 10

The game's goal is to get a value closest to 21 it without going over (busting). If the player busts, they lose.

Once a player's turn is over, they can no longer choose to draw another card.

Double downs are allowed. A player can only double down with their starting hand. They double their bet and are given only one hit and then are forced to stand.

The House is forced to draw a card if they're under 16 and are forced to stay at hard 17.

Natural blackjacks have a payout of 2.5x. Natural blackjack beats dealers unnatural blackjack. Ties to dealer natural.

Bets limits: 50k minimum to 500k maximum. Members may place bets up to 750k. Membership+ members up to 1.5mil max bet!

On the occasion of a tie between the player and the House, the bet will be "pushed" to the next round, unless the player asks for it back.

Upon the player's victory, the House retains 5% of the total winnings. That's the House fee. Example - you bet 250k and win, the house would pay you x2 of your bet (500k) minus 5 percent, so they'd pay you 487,500 for winning!
